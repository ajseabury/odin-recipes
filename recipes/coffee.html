Coffee

Description

A simple recipe for black coffee, easy and delicous.

Ingredients

  • Ground coffee
  • Water
  • Cup or mug (can also use bowl if needed in a pinch
  • Strainer

Steps

  1. Boil water to a rolling boil.
  2. Put ground coffee in cup, mug, or bowl.
  3. Pour boiling water over coffee.
  4. Let sit for 5 minutes, then strain grounds.
  5. Drink coffee.
