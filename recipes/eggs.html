Eggs

Description

A simple guide to eggs (safe for beginners!)

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Spray oil
  • Salt and Pepper (S&P)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, start with two if this is your first time (with practice you can gradually add more eggs as tolerated).
  2. Take any fork from your kitchen drawer and slap eggs back and forth until yolks liquid.
  3. Heat pan on medium heat, then spray oil into pan when hot (light coating).
  4. Pour liquid eggs into pan.
  5. Using spatula, fold eggs over and chop to preferred consistency.
  6. Add S&P to taste. Beginners beware of salt, use caution. Pepper is safe.
