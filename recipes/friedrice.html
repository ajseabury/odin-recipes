Fried Rice

Description

Super easy and simple fried rice, also delicious.

Ingredients

  • Minute Rice
  • Egg
  • Olive oil or any oil you have on hand
  • Onion, diced
  • Garlic, smashed

Steps

  1. Dice onion to small bits.
  2. Smash garlic with blade of knife then dice.
  3. Pour 1/2 TBSP olive oil in pan, heat pan on medium.
  4. Pour onion and garlic mixture onto hot oil and stir frequently to avoid sticking.
  5. Once onions and garlic soft and brown, spread to one corner of pan for storage.
  6. Pour 1-2 TBSP olive oil in pan and heat on high.
  7. Heat minute rice while oil is heating.
  8. When oil is hot, pour rice into oil and stir rapidly distributing hot oil onto rice.
  9. Take egg and crack over rice, incorporate rice, egg, onion, and garlic into one pile.
  10. When egg is cooked into rice pile, S&P to taste, soy sauce optional.
